Gluten Free Pancakes

Description

A wonderful recipe for Pancakes that are gluten free, egg free, and dairy free

Ingredients

  • Gluten free flour
  • Bananas
  • Coconut milk
  • Sugar
  • Butter substitute
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Combine flour, baking powder and sugar in large mixing bowl.
  2. Mash bananas in separate bowl.
  3. Pour in coconut milk, vanilla extract and melted butter substitute.
  4. Combine wet and dry Ingredients. Mix until well combined. (Don't worry about overmixing since it's gluten free it won't get tough)
  5. On medium low heat in medium sized saucepan melt butter substitute.
  6. Pour in desired amount of pancake mix into pan, flip when sides look solid.
  7. Serve with butter substitute, powdered sugar, maple syrup and other toppings of choice.
