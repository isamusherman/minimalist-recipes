Steak

About

Steak is a fantastic source of protein and tastes delicious. Here is how to make the perfect steak every time!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Let steak rest outside of fridge until it reaches room temperature
  2. Salt and pepper the steak
  3. Crush cloves of garlic with side of a knife
  4. Heat pan (preferably cast iron) on medium heat
  5. Add butter to pan
  6. Once butter is hot, add garlic and thyme to pan
  7. Add steak to pan, flipping after 1-2 minute and rubbing garlic and thyme all over steak
  8. When steak is firm and springs back, take out of pan
  9. Let steak rest for at least one minute before serving
